**Description**

DC Motor-25 Bracket B-Blue\(Pair\) is the update version of DC Motor-25 Bracket -Blue\(Pair\).

**Features**

* Made from 6061 aluminum, 3mm thick, anodized surface.
* Compatible to 37mm DC Motor.
* Sold in Pair.
